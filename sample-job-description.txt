Senior AI/ML Engineer

Company: AI Innovations Corp
Location: Remote
Type: Full-time

About the Role:
We are seeking a talented Senior AI/ML Engineer to join our growing team. You will be responsible for 
designing, developing, and deploying machine learning models and AI solutions that power our products.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and implement machine learning models for production environments

- Deploy models to cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI solutions
- Monitor model performance and implement improvements
- Mentor junior team members on ML best practices
- Stay current with latest AI/ML research and technologies

Technical Stack:
